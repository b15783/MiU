      WoW Character Tracker

      Thank you for using my World of Warcraft Character Tracker. This is a mobile app that you can use to keep track of and display all of your WoW characters.

      Code for this app can be viewed on my GITHUB page.

      News

      Hotfixes for June 10th

      In the Throne of Thunder, Lei Shen will now always drop a Titan Runestone of Secrets of the Empire for players on the legendary quest line.

      Hey hunters! Arcane Shot, Cobra Shot and Steady Shot will all deal 10% more damage after that realm restart.

      Durumu and his minions have got us all figured out. Elixirs of Detect Invisibility will no longer allow players to see fog beasts while they are invisible.

      New Battle.net services activated

      You no longer have to login to WoW, SC2, or D3, as clicking play will automatically log you in.

      A list of you BattleTag and RealID friends is now shown in a small window in the client.

      Chat is disabled for this phase of testing, but you will be able to chat with you BattleTag and RealID friends in the future.
